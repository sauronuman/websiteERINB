Qualifications & Requirements

To become a Web Developer full time, you will need specific knowledge and qualifications. This includes:

  • GCSE in IT/BTEC Equivalent
  • Level 2 award in Creating a Website
  • Level 2 Certificate in Interactive Media
  • Level 2/3 Diploma in Information Technology
  • Level 3 Diploma in IT User Skills










 Key skills to be successful in web development include:

  • Computer literacy
  • Strong numeracy skills
  • Strong creative ability
  • Attention to detail
  • Strong communication skills
  • Excellent problem-solving skills
  • A logical approach to work
  • The ability to explain technical matters clearly
  • A keen interest in technology
